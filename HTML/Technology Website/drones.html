Drones

Dones help in many different ways. While people may be terrified of these remotely-piloted aircrafts, they don't realize how much they can help. One use is for photography. Drones can help photographers take amazing aerial photos and videos. You may not realize it, but drones are used a lot in films today. Another use is package delivery. Amazon is developing drone technology ("Amazon Prime Air") to deliver lightweight packages locally. They had their first flight on December 7, 2016. Finally, there is emergency services. A student attending Delft University of Technology (TU Delft) had an idea for an ambulance drone, a "toolbox for your emergency supplies."
