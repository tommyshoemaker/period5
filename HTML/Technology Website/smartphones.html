Smartphones

Smartphones are a great way for people to communicate. With things like texting, video chat, and of course calling, these little devices can pack a lot of power. The first "smartphone", the Simon Personal Computer, made by IBM in 1992. The first mainstream smartphone was the iPhone, released in 2007. Smartphone can help with a lot of things. There are a lot of hidden features you may not know about in your phone. For example iPhones can take your heart rate accurately with an app, through your camera. Some parents may think that their child is not responsible enough for a phone, but there are many reasons why they should get one. For example, there is an emergency SOS feature on the iPhone and Apple Watch. They will call 911 and send them your location. Thankfully, I have never had to use it. Without phones, our lives would be a lot more complicated.
